Our Story

Where It All Began

Urbest Eggs didn't start as a business — it started as a commitment. A promise to raise hens the right way, collect every egg by hand, and bring something real to your table.

A Farm Born from Care

Nestled in the heart of Pangasinan, our farm began with a single flock and a simple belief: that the quality of an egg is a direct reflection of how its hen is treated. Every hen at Urbest is raised in open, well-ventilated spaces — never caged, never rushed.

We started small, selling only to neighbors and local markets. Word spread not because of advertising, but because the difference was undeniable. The yolks were richer. The shells were stronger. The taste was something people kept coming back for.

The Pangasinan Advantage

Pangasinan's climate is ideal for layer farming. The combination of natural airflow, rich soil for foraging, and the dedication of local hands means our hens thrive in conditions most commercial farms can't replicate. It's not just geography — it's a way of life passed down through generations of farmers who understood that patience produces quality.
