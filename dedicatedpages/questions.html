Ordering

Browse our egg sizes on the main page, select how many trays you'd like, and add them to your cart. Once you're ready, proceed to checkout and we'll handle the rest.

Our minimum order is one tray (30 eggs). For bulk or reseller orders, feel free to reach out through our Contact page and we'll work something out.

Orders can be modified or cancelled before they are packed and dispatched. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to make a change and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

Delivery

We currently deliver across Pangasinan and to select nearby areas. If you're unsure whether we cover your location, send us a message through the Contact page and we'll let you know.

Most orders within our main delivery area arrive the same day or next day after packing. We'll notify you once your order is on its way.

Each tray is carefully packed to minimize movement and prevent breakage during transit. We take pride in the condition your eggs arrive in — if anything arrives broken, let us know.
